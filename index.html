Featured Project

799 American Nursing Homes Received Abuse Alerts in 2019

A nursing home is a place for the elderly who can’t be cared for at home and need 24-hour nursing care. However, too many cases of elder abuse in nursing homes have consistently got reported and remained unaddressed in the U.S. It is time for all of us to take a close look and take action!

This "Nursing Homes with Abuse Alerts App", independently developed on my own by R shiny, contains detailed information of 11,994 Medicare and Medicaid certified nursing home nationwide and lays emphasis on 799 nursing homes which have been flagged with abuse alerts.

Project 2

What's New Yorkers favorite Airbnb room?

New York City (NYC) has one of the most active Airbnb markets in the United States with more than 100,000 properties flooding into the city since 2018. Generally, Airbnb provides three types of rooms: entire home or apartment, private room and shared room. Listing prices and counts vary across room types and have been affected by availability and review frequency. Accordingly, this analysis is aimed at exploring listing prices by each room types.

Project 3

Predictive Policing: Shooting avoidance

For years, gun violence in Philadelphia is an epidemic that the Police Department is battling. Building predictive policing model is one of tries the Department have made to tackle the dire problem. Inspired by previous efforts and present Black Lives Matters demonstrations, this analysis makes effort to develop a predictive policing model for the shootings in Philadelphia with the goal of achieving accurate prediction and robust generalizability, as well as reducing systematic racism when it comes to gun violence.

Project 4

TOD Study in Dallas, TX

Transit-Oriented Development, or TOD for short, is at the heart of the City of Dallas’ long-range land use plan, forwardDallas!. Dallas is home to 74 passenger rail stations in the system of Dallas Area Rapid Transit (DART), and each presenting a unique opportunity to develop a new mixed-use neighborhood. This project is aimed at provide an overview on whether residents would like to pay more for the sake of the potential benefits from TOD in Dallas.

Project 5

Bikeshare system rebalancing within the DC Metro area

Capital Bikeshare (CaBi) was one of the first big public bikeshare systems in the United State offering bike rental service within the DC Metro area. The program offers bikes for rental (by single-ride or membership) at nearly 700 stations in DC and Arlington, VA (and more in more distant cities). One of the biggest objectives of bikesharing systems is to make it possible that people can bike without owning their own bicycle at the expense of a small subscription fee.
